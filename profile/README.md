We're a small, hands-on shop that tunes and deploys AI models, modernizes legacy systems, and turns real workflows into working pilots. No strategy decks. No "AI transformation roadmaps." Just shipped software your team can use on Monday.

## What we do

### 🌱 Train — 2 to 6 weeks
Executive briefings and AI readiness curriculum that meet teams where they are.
- Half-day executive briefings
- Six-week leadership cohorts
- Role-specific, hands-on workshops

### 🛠️ Pilot — 3 weeks
Fixed scope, fixed price. We pick one real workflow and ship a working agent for it, plus a decision memo on what it would take to deploy.

### 🚀 Deploy — 2 to 6 months
Production work: model fine-tuning, agentic systems, and legacy migrations using modern coding harnesses.
